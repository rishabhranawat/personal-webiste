Sequencing Educational Content

18th August 2018

High Stakes Domains: In such domains, it is infeasible to evaluate candidate representations by running them online. Instead, one must leverage existing data to learn and evaluate new policies for future use.

Sequencing educational content so that the student gets the most out of it. This is a challenging reinforcement learning problem, since the agent must infer meaning from the high-dimensional history of the student's past behavior, many features of which are irrelevant to building a good policy. This problem is also partially observable, since we cannot access the true mental state of the student, only observed behaviours.

Predictive State Representations may be capable of capturing the environment, learning all the parameters of a complex PSR os POMDP model directly may not always be the best approach given limited data e.g., due to overfitting.
